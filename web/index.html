What is PCS Scout?

A free house-hunting tool for Military Service Members and Families making a PCS move — built on official public data. No account, no ads, no tracking.

  1. Enter your duty location to see your official BAH.
  2. Set a rent budget at, under, or over your allowance.
  3. Tap the map to compare commutes — including rush hour — and see nearby schools. Add a spouse's workplace to find the area that's fair to both drives.
  4. Jump to listing sites pre-filtered to your area, budget, and home size.
